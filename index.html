Explanation of using WikiCategories

Every article on Wikipedia is placed into groups, or categories. For instance, the article on "Stranger Things" is placed into the category of "American science fiction television series" among others (10 to be exact). By typing in "Stranger Things" and clicking 'Submit' below, you will find all the Wikipedia categories for "Stranger Things". THIS IS A WORK STILL IN PROGRESS
